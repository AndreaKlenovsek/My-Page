About me

Andrea Klenovšek

My name is Andrea Klenovšek, I finished marketing master’s degree at the Faculty of Economics and Business in Maribor. Initially, my interest was in digital advertising (social networks and SEA - Google Ads). However, later on, during my work and independent learning, I realized that my so-called "true love" lies in design, more precisely web design. During my studies and work, I used programs such as InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, and later on I began learning HTML, CSS and Sass. My next goals are to improve my knowledge in the mentioned field and to become qualified in JavaScript.
